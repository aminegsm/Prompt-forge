// About PromptForge

The Ultimate Free AI Image Toolkit

PromptForge is a comprehensive, browser-based suite of AI image tools designed for creators, designers, and prompt engineers. Unlike other platforms that require server uploads and charge subscription fees, all our tools run entirely in your browser using advanced HTML5 Canvas APIs. This means your data is 100% private and processing is instant.

Our flagship tool, the Image to Prompt Generator, analyzes the pixel data of any image to reverse-engineer the perfect text prompt. Whether you are trying to mimic a photographic style for Midjourney, replicate a color palette in Stable Diffusion, or understand the lighting setup of a reference image, our engine extracts key attributes like brightness, saturation, edge density, and dominant colors to build highly accurate prompts.

Remove Objects from Photos Online

Need to clean up a photo? Our AI Object Eraser tool lets you brush over unwanted elements—whether it's a photobomber, a stray wire, or a blemish—and intelligently fills the background using content-aware interpolation algorithms. It's a free, fast alternative to expensive photo editing software.

Convert and Resize Images for Free

Beyond AI prompts, PromptForge includes a lightweight Image Format Converter (PNG, JPG, WebP) with adjustable compression, and a Smart Image Resizer to help you prepare assets for web upload or print. No watermarks, no file limits, completely free.
